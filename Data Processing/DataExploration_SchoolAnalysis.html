Problem statement:¶

Suppose you are a public school admin. Some schools in your state of Tennessee are performing below average academically. Your superintendent under pressure from frustrated parents annd voters approached you with task of understanding why these schoolds are under performing. To improve school performance, you need to learn more about these schools and their students

Objective:¶

Perform exploratory data analysis which includes determining the type of data correlation analysis.

  1. Read Data from file
  2. Describe the data to find more details.
  3. Find the correlation between 'reduced lunch' and 'school rating'
